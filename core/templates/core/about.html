Our Heritage

Where traditions are as old as the mountains, and every thread carries a legacy

Born in the Valleys

At Oraagh, every thread carries a legacy, every design whispers a tale, and every creation is a tribute to timeless elegance.

Born in the valleys where traditions are as old as the mountains, Oraagh was founded with a simple vision: to preserve the art of handmade craftsmanship while bringing its beauty to the modern world.

Our artisans, hailing from regions known for their centuries-old weaving techniques, pour their heart and soul into every shawl, muffler, stole, and purse. Each piece is not just an accessory—it's a canvas of culture, a story of heritage, and a mark of individuality.

Our Philosophy

We believe fashion is more than appearance; it is memory, meaning, and connection. When you wrap an Oraagh shawl around your shoulders or carry one of our handmade purses, you don't just wear an item—you embrace generations of artistry, patience, and perfection.

Our collections are carefully curated to reflect both tradition and contemporary charm. From the warmth of Islampuri woolen shawls to the delicacy of handcrafted stoles, Oraagh is where heritage meets modern elegance.

Our Mission

With our commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and the revival of cultural craftsmanship, Oraagh is not just a brand. It is a movement—to honor artisans, to revive traditions, and to bring you treasures that never fade with time.
